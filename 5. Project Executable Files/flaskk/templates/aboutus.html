About Us

Welcome to our Liver Cirrhosis Detection platform! Our team, comprising Saiprasad Pandilwar, Chandrakala K, Niranchna Natarajan and Gopika Nambiar, has developed an advanced machine learning model designed to assist in the early detection of liver cirrhosis. By analyzing a variety of blood parameters, our model can provide valuable insights that may help in the diagnosis and management of this serious condition.

Our mission is to leverage the power of technology to improve healthcare. We aim to provide a reliable and accessible tool for medical practitioners to aid in the early detection of liver cirrhosis, ultimately enhancing the quality of care and patient well-being.
